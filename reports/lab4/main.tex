\subsection{Параметри хвилі}

Довжина хвилі:
$\lambda = c/f = 1480/23000 \approx 64{,}3$~мм.

Розмір елемента сітки: $h_{\max} \leq \lambda/10 \approx 6{,}4$~мм.

Часовий крок: $\Delta t \leq 1/(20f) \approx 2{,}17 \times 10^{-6}$~с.

Гармонічний рух випромінювача:
\begin{equation}
  z(t) = A\,\sin(2\pi f\, t) = 1{,}3 \times 10^{-3}\,\sin(2\pi \times 23000 \times t)~\text{[мм]}.
\end{equation}

\section{Послідовність виконання}
% ===================================================================

Робота виконана через ANSYS Workbench Journaling (\texttt{lab4.wbjn}) з солвером CFX.

\begin{lstlisting}[style=scheme]
1. CreateSystem("Fluid Flow (CFX)")
2. Import lab4.step (1 body: stadium bath with recess)
3. Enter Meshing
   |-- Print face centroids (diagnostic)
   |-- NS "Emitter" -> face at Z~5mm, centroid at origin,
   |                    area ~ 1257mm^2
   |-- NS "Walls"   -> everything else
   |-- Mesh 3mm (lambda/10=6.4mm, we use lambda/21)
   |   -> mesh.png
4. Enter CFX-Pre
   |-- Import CCL with LIBRARY section:
   |   MATERIAL CompressibleWater:
   |     EOS = Linear Elastic, c=1480 m/s, rho=998.2 kg/m^3
   |   EXPRESSIONS:
   |     freq=23kHz, amp=1.3um, disp_z=amp*sin(2*pi*f*t)
   |   FluidDomain: CompressibleWater (General Material),
   |     Laminar
   |     Mesh Deformation = Regions of Motion Specified
   |     Emitter: Specified Displacement Z=disp_z
   |     Walls: Stationary, No Slip
   |   Transient: dt=2.17us, T=0.01s (~4600 steps)
   |   Output: P,V every 10 steps -> 460 frames
   |   Parallel: -part 12
5. Solve
6. Enter CFX-Post
   |-- Plane: XZ at Y=0
   |-- Export: pressure, velocity, geometry
   '-- Export 50 key frames -> ffmpeg -> animation.mp4
\end{lstlisting}
