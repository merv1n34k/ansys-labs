\subsection{Ключові формули}

Закон Фур'є: $q = -\lambda\,\partial T / \partial x$.
Рівняння енергії: $\rho c_p(\partial T/\partial t + \mathbf{U}\cdot\nabla T) = \nabla\cdot(\lambda\nabla T) + S_E$.
Умова спряження (CHT) на інтерфейсі Fluid--Solid:
\begin{equation}
  T_{\mathrm{solid}}\big|_{\mathrm{int}} = T_{\mathrm{fluid}}\big|_{\mathrm{int}},
  \qquad
  q_{\mathrm{solid}}\big|_{\mathrm{int}} = q_{\mathrm{fluid}}\big|_{\mathrm{int}}.
\end{equation}

Робота виконана через ANSYS Workbench Journaling (\texttt{lab3.wbjn}) з солвером CFX.

\begin{lstlisting}[style=scheme]
1. CreateSystem("Fluid Flow (CFX)")
2. Import lab3.step (3 bodies: vessel_fluid,
   coil_solid_copper, coil_fluid)
3. Enter Meshing
   |-- ID bodies by volume: vessel > coil_solid > coil_fluid
   |-- Print body names (verify match CCL Location refs)
   |-- Nozzle detection: face at max X (~310),
   |   near target Z, small area
   |-- NS "Vessel_Inlet"   -> vessel face at Z~950, X~310
   |-- NS "Vessel_Outlet"  -> vessel face at Z~50,  X~310
   |-- NS "Vessel_Wall"    -> remaining vessel faces
   |-- NS "Circuit_Inlet"  -> coil_fluid face Z~183, X~310
   |-- NS "Circuit_Outlet" -> coil_fluid face Z~817, X~310
   |-- Inflation -> scoped to Vessel_Wall, 3 layers,
   |               h1=0.5mm
   |-- Mesh 5mm -> mesh.png
4. Enter CFX-Pre
   |-- Import CCL (domains only, NO explicit interfaces):
   |   VesselFluid: Location=vessel_fluid, Water,
   |     Total Energy, k-eps
   |     Inlet: 285K (12C), 0.006 m/s
   |     Outlet: 0Pa | Wall: Adiabatic
   |   CoilSolid: Location=coil_solid_copper,
   |     Copper, Thermal Energy
   |   CoilFluid: Location=coil_fluid, Water,
   |     Total Energy, k-eps
   |     Inlet: 381K (108C), 1.4 m/s | Outlet: 0Pa
   |   Solver: High Res, 500 iter, RMS 1e-4
   |-- > autoconnect  (auto-detect Fluid-Solid interfaces)
5. Solve
6. Enter CFX-Post
   |-- Plane: XZ at Y=0
   |-- Export: temp_vessel, temp_circuit,
   |           vel_vessel, vel_circuit
   |-- areaAve(T) and areaAve(V) at all 4 boundaries
   '-- geometry.png
\end{lstlisting}
